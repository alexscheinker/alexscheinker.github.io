Welcome

Welcome to my research page. I work on generative AI, adaptive control theory, and how to combine them to make robust tools for complex time-varying dynamic systems. Applications include virtual 6D beam diagnostics for charged particle beams in particle accelerators, autonomous particle accelerators, and 3D dynamic imaging.

About Me

I have a PhD in Control Theory and Masters degrees in Math and Physics. I am the Adaptive Machine Learning Team Leader at Los Alamos National Laboratory and a Research Affiliate at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory.
